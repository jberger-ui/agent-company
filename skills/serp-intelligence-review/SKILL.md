# SERP Intelligence Review

## Purpose

Create a concise but decision-ready review of where the company should place its next editorial bets.

## Instructions

1. Review target themes, entities, and reader problems.
2. Identify what looks durable versus time-sensitive.
3. Flag saturated topics where another generic post is unlikely to help.
4. Surface specific differentiation opportunities.
5. Recommend what should be produced, updated, merged, or avoided.

## Output format

- key opportunities
- commodity areas to avoid
- answer-engine opportunities
- recommended content bets
- evidence or rationale
