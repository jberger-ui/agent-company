## Objective

Increase the share of published content that is easy for answer engines and retrieval systems to understand, cite, and trust.

## Success criteria

- drafts answer a concrete reader problem directly
- entity definitions and evidence are clearer
- overlap reviews catch weak commodity angles before publication
